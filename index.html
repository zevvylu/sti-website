Safe sex tips

  • Condoms and dental dams for oral sex are higly effective and the easiest method of preventing STI's. At the SHQ clinc, free condoms and dental dams are available!

  • Anonymous text services are available if you contract an STI. Contracting STI's isnt the end of the world! Most are highly treatable with medication. Early testing is key!
